My favourite food is Rice and plantain

About the food origin

Rice is probably a descendent of wild grass that was most likely cultivated in the foothills of the far Eastern Himalayas. Another school of thought believes that the rice plant may have originated in southern India, then spread to the north of the country and then onwards to China.

Plantains are believed to have originated in Southeast Asia. Two groups of plantains are thought to have a common origin: the horn plantain and the French plantain. Both types grow in India, Africa, Egypt, and tropical America. The French plantains also occur in Indonesia and the islands of the Pacific.

Ingredients

Items Measurement
Rice 3 Cups of Rice

Vegetable oil 1 bottle of oil
Method of preparing the meal

Cooking Rice

  • First , wash the rice throughly in warm water to remove the starch content.
  • Afterwards, boil some water and pour the washed rice inside the boiling water.
  • Add one table spoon of salt
  • Allow it to boil for 20 minutes
  • Taste the rice to check if it is soft enough
  • If not soft enough, cook for another few minutes e.g 3 minutes
  • If it is soft enough, then you can stop cooking

Frying Plantain

  • Remove the plantain peel
  • Slice to your desire sizes and shapes
  • Allow the oil to be hot
  • Holdon for some times
  • Allow it to deep fry then turn to the other side
  • The plantain is ready
